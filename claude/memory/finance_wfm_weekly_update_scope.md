The Finance/WFM weekly Monday Slack update covers **Consumer (Cx) and Dasher (Dx) only** — in-house HC, NH classes, and BPO FTE movements.

**Excluded LOBs (never mention):**
- Merchant / Mx: T1 Mx, T3 Mx, Mx Menu, Mx POS, Mx VIP, MxP/MSS, GA Mx
- Tablets (explicitly excluded Apr 13: "exclude Menu, POS, Tablets, T1 Mx, MXP/MSS, GA Mx")
- Integrity LOBs are **INCLUDED** in the AOP comparison table with these names:
  - Critical Investigations, Safety (=CR/Community Response), Authenticity (=MQ/Marketplace Quality), Compliance (=CD/Community Defense, PHX-only; CDMX=0), Fraud
  - Finance Tab rows: Crit Inv CDMX=39 PHX=76; Safety CDMX=116 PHX=110; Authenticity CDMX=117 PHX=111; Compliance PHX=112 (row 118 CDMX=0); Fraud PHX=124 CDMX=127
- Drive: DoorDash Drive LOB (both PHX and CDMX), Drive LOF
- Deprecated / inactive LOBs

**Why:** These LOBs are out of scope for this specific weekly Finance sync. Scope is Cx + Dx in-house and BPO only.

**How to apply:** When composing or editing the weekly #support-finance-working-group message:
- Only surface Cx and Dx LOB changes (in-house HC, NH classes, BPO queues)
- Strip any Mx, MSS, Integrity, or Drive references before finalizing
- For BPO section, only include Cx and Dx vendor queues (e.g., TU Dx Non-Live, Alorica Dx VIP)
- Tablets is explicitly excluded even though it appears in the PHX LOB list
